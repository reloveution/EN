ТИПИЧНЫЕ ВЫРАЖЕНИЯ

✅ be + adjective: be sure, be ready, be afraid, be aware, be busy
✅ be + noun/pronoun: be a teacher, be his friend
✅ be worth / be worth it (стоит того, заслуживает внимания)  
✅ be in charge of / be responsible for (быть ответственным за, руководить)  
✅ be up to (It’s up to you.) — зависит от…, решать тебе
✅ be about to (собираться)
✅ be supposed to (предполагалось)
✅ be late / be early (опоздать / прийти раньше времени)
✅ Повелительное наклонение: Be careful! Be quiet! Be yourself!

ЧАСТЫЕ ОШИБКИ

❌ I not happy → ✅ I am not happy.
❌ Are there is a problem? → ✅ Is there a problem?
❌ There’s many people → ✅ There are many people.
❌ He’s teacher → ✅ He’s a teacher.
❌ They was here → ✅ They were here.
❌ I am agree → ✅ I agree.
❌ The news are bad → ✅ The news is bad.
❌ He is married? → ✅ Is he married?
❌ Who is he? He is doctor → ✅ He is a doctor.
❌ It’s happens → ✅ It happens.
❌ I am go → ✅ I am going / I go (по ситуации).
❌ She was went → ✅ She went / She was going.

ПРАКТИКА

ЧАСТЬ 1: Переведите
1. Мы были счастливы.
2. Это будет сложно.
3. Я собираюсь быть осторожным.
4. Здесь нет времени на разговоры.
5. Там был парк.

ОТВЕТЫ:
1. We were happy.
2. It will be difficult.
3. I am going to be careful.
4. There is no time for talking.
5. There used to be a park there.

ЧАСТЬ 2: Заполните формы to be
1. ___ she a doctor? — Yes, she ___.
2. They ___ not ready.
3. ___ there three reasons?
4. How long ___ it been raining?
5. The keys ___ on the table.

ОТВЕТЫ:
1. Is she a doctor? — Yes, she is.
2. are (They are not ready.)
3. Are (Are there three reasons?)
4. has (How long has it been raining?)
5. are (The keys are on the table.)

ЧАСТЬ 3: Исправьте ошибки
1. Their is a problem.
2. He’s be late.
3. They was here yesterday.
4. The news are good.
5. There’s many advantages.

ОТВЕТЫ:
1. There is a problem.
2. He’ll be late / He is going to be late.
3. They were here yesterday.
4. The news is good.
5. There are many advantages.

ЧАСТЬ 4: Составьте
1. Вопрос: She is my friend.
2. Отрицание: We are ready.
3. Экзистенциальное: problem / solutions / time.
4. Предложение с it is + прилагательное + to V.
5. Предложение с there seems to be.

Возможные ответы:
1. Is she my friend?
2. We are not ready. / We aren’t ready.
3. There is a problem. / There are solutions. / There is no time.
4. It is important to practice every day.
5. There seems to be a misunderstanding.
