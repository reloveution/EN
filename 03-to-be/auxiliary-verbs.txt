🔄 HAVE / HAS / HAD

⚠️ Двойная функция:
    1. Основной глагол "иметь" (have = обладать, иметь)
    2. Вспомогательный глагол для Perfect времён

––––––––––––––––––––––––––––––––––––––––––––––––––––––

📊 СХЕМА: HAVE КАК ОСНОВНОЙ ГЛАГОЛ
УТВЕРЖДЕНИЯ | ВОПРОСЫ | ОТРИЦАНИЯ

PAST (Past Simple)
    AFFIRMATIVE: I/You/He/She/It/We/They had
        I had a problem. / They had a meeting. / She had dinner.
    
    INVERSION QUESTION: Did + подлежащее + have?
        Did I have...? / Did you have lunch? / Did they have problems? / Did she have a car?
    
    NEGATIVE: I/You/He/She/It/We/They didn't have
        I didn't have breakfast. / They didn't have time. / She didn't have the key.

PRES (Present Simple)
    AFFIRMATIVE:
        I/You/We/They have → I have a car. / They have time. / You have a pen.
        He/She/It has → She has a dog. / He has money. / It has batteries.
    
    INVERSION QUESTION: Do/Does + подлежащее + have?
        Do I/you/we/they have...? → Do you have a pen? / Do they have time?
        Does he/she/it have...? → Does she have a cat? / Does it have batteries?
    
    NEGATIVE:
        I/You/We/They don't have → I don't have time. / They don't have money.
        He/She/It doesn't have → He doesn't have a car. / She doesn't have the key.

FUTR (Future Simple)
    AFFIRMATIVE: I/You/He/She/It/We/They will have
        I will have time tomorrow. / She will have a new job. / They will have questions.
    
    INVERSION QUESTION: Will + подлежащее + have?
        Will I/you/he/she/it/we/they have...? → Will you have time? / Will they have questions?
    
    NEGATIVE: I/You/He/She/It/We/They won't have
        I won't have time tomorrow. / She won't have a chance. / They won't have problems.

📊 СХЕМА: HAVE КАК ВСПОМОГАТЕЛЬНЫЙ (PERFECT)
УТВЕРЖДЕНИЯ | ВОПРОСЫ | ОТРИЦАНИЯ
Схема: have/has/had + V3

PAST (Past Perfect)
    AFFIRMATIVE: I/You/He/She/It/We/They had + V3
        I had finished. / They had left before I arrived. / She had called.
    
    INVERSION QUESTION: Had + подлежащее + V3?
        Had I/you/he/she/it/we/they done...? → Had you finished? / Had they left? / Had she called?
    
    NEGATIVE: I/You/He/She/It/We/They hadn't + V3
        I hadn't seen him. / They hadn't arrived. / She hadn't finished.
        hadn't = had not

PRES (Present Perfect)
    AFFIRMATIVE:
        I/You/We/They have + V3 → I have finished. / They have left. / You have seen.
        He/She/It has + V3 → She has arrived. / He has called. / It has stopped.
    
    INVERSION QUESTION: Have/Has + подлежащее + V3?
        Have I/you/we/they done...? → Have you finished? / Have they arrived?
        Has he/she/it done...? → Has she called? / Has it stopped?
    
    NEGATIVE:
        I/You/We/They haven't + V3 → I haven't seen him. / They haven't arrived.
        He/She/It hasn't + V3 → She hasn't called. / He hasn't finished.
        haven't = have not | hasn't = has not

FUTR (Future Perfect)
    AFFIRMATIVE: I/You/He/She/It/We/They will have + V3
        I will have finished by 5 PM. / She will have arrived by then. / They will have left.
    
    INVERSION QUESTION: Will + подлежащее + have + V3?
        Will I/you/he/she/it/we/they have done...? → Will you have finished? / Will they have left?
    
    NEGATIVE: I/You/He/She/It/We/They won't have + V3
        I won't have finished by then. / They won't have arrived. / She won't have called.

✅ HAVE как ОСНОВНОЙ глагол (иметь, обладать)

Формы по лицам и временам:
    • Настоящее (Present Simple):
        – I/You/We/They have → I have a car. / They have time.
        – He/She/It has → She has a dog. / He has money.
    
    • Прошедшее (Past Simple):
        – I/You/He/She/It/We/They had → I had a problem. / They had a meeting.

    • Будущее (Future Simple):
        – will have → I will have time tomorrow. / She will have a new job.

Вопросы (нужен do/does/did):
    • Настоящее:
        – Do you have...? → Do you have a pen? / Do they have time?
        – Does he/she/it have...? → Does she have a cat? / Does it have batteries?
    
    • Прошедшее:
        – Did I/you/he/she/it/we/they have...? → Did you have lunch? / Did they have problems?

    • Будущее:
        – Will I/you/he/she/it/we/they have...? → Will you have time? / Will they have questions?

Отрицания (нужен do/does/did + not):
    • Настоящее:
        – I/You/We/They don't have → I don't have time. / They don't have money.
        – He/She/It doesn't have → He doesn't have a car. / She doesn't have the key.
    
    • Прошедшее:
        – I/You/He/She/It/We/They didn't have → I didn't have breakfast. / They didn't have time.

    • Будущее:
        – won't have → I won't have time tomorrow. / She won't have a chance.

✅ HAVE как ВСПОМОГАТЕЛЬНЫЙ глагол (Perfect времена)

Схема: have/has/had + V3 (третья форма глагола)

    • Present Perfect: have/has + V3
        Утверждения:
            – I/You/We/They have done → I have finished. / They have left.
            – He/She/It has done → She has arrived. / He has called.

        Вопросы (инверсия: have/has + подлежащее):
            – Have I/you/we/they done...? → Have you finished? / Have they arrived?
            – Has he/she/it done...? → Has she called? / Has it stopped?

        Отрицания:
            – I/You/We/They haven't done → I haven't seen him. / They haven't arrived.
            – He/She/It hasn't done → She hasn't called. / He hasn't finished.
            – haven't = have not | hasn't = has not

    • Past Perfect: had + V3
        Утверждения:
            – I/You/He/She/It/We/They had done → I had finished. / They had left before I arrived.

        Вопросы (инверсия: had + подлежащее):
            – Had I/you/he/she/it/we/they done...? → Had you finished? / Had they left?

        Отрицания:
            – I/You/He/She/It/We/They hadn't done → I hadn't seen him. / They hadn't arrived.
            – hadn't = had not

    • Future Perfect: will have + V3
        Утверждения:
            – will have done → I will have finished by 5 PM. / She will have arrived by then.

        Вопросы:
            – Will I/you/he/she/it/we/they have done...? → Will you have finished? / Will they have left?

        Отрицания:
            – won't have done → I won't have finished by then. / They won't have arrived.

📊 СХЕМА: DO КАК ВСПОМОГАТЕЛЬНЫЙ (только PRES и PAST)
УТВЕРЖДЕНИЯ | ВОПРОСЫ | ОТРИЦАНИЯ
⚠️ После do/does/did глагол ВСЕГДА в начальной форме (без -s, -ed, -ing)

PAST (Past Simple)
    AFFIRMATIVE: подлежащее + V (обычная форма)
        I worked. / They came yesterday. / She saw him.
    
    INVERSION QUESTION: Did + подлежащее + V?
        Did I/you/he/she/it/we/they work? → Did you see him? / Did they come? / Did she go?
        Wh- + did + подлежащее + V? → Where did you go? / What did she say?
    
    NEGATIVE: I/You/He/She/It/We/They didn't + V
        I didn't see him. / They didn't come. / She didn't go.
        didn't = did not

PRES (Present Simple)
    AFFIRMATIVE:
        I/You/We/They + V → I work here. / They like coffee. / You live here.
        He/She/It + V-s → She speaks English. / He likes pizza. / It works.
    
    INVERSION QUESTION: Do/Does + подлежащее + V?
        Do I/you/we/they work? → Do you work here? / Do they like coffee?
        Does he/she/it work? → Does she speak English? / Does it work?
        Wh- + do/does + подлежащее + V? → Where do you live? / What does she do?
    
    NEGATIVE:
        I/You/We/They don't + V → I don't smoke. / They don't live here.
        He/She/It doesn't + V → She doesn't speak French. / He doesn't like it.
        don't = do not | doesn't = does not

📝 УСИЛЕНИЕ (эмфаза) - используется do/does/did в утверждениях:
    – I DO like pizza! → Я ДЕЙСТВИТЕЛЬНО люблю пиццу!
    – She DOES speak English. → Она ДЕЙСТВИТЕЛЬНО говорит по-английски.
    – I DID finish my homework. → Я ДЕЙСТВИТЕЛЬНО закончил домашку.
    – DO come in! → Обязательно заходи! (вежливая просьба)

⚠️ ВАЖНЫЕ ПРАВИЛА И ИСКЛЮЧЕНИЯ

1. Have как основной глагол = нужен do/does/did:
    – Do you have time? (не *Have you time?* в американском варианте)
    – She doesn't have a car.
    – Did they have lunch?

2. Have как вспомогательный = НЕ нужен do/does/did:
    – Have you finished? (не *Do you have finished?*)
    – She hasn't arrived yet.
    – Had they left before you came?

3. Be, модальные глаголы (can, must, may) НЕ используют do/does/did:
    – Are you ready? (не *Do you be ready?*)
    – Can you help? (не *Do you can help?*)
    – Must she go? (не *Does she must go?*)

4. После do/does/did глагол БЕЗ окончаний:
    – Do you work? (не *Do you works?*)
    – Does she go? (не *Does she goes?*)
    – Did they see? (не *Did they saw?*)

5. Сокращения:
    – have not → haven't
    – has not → hasn't
    – had not → hadn't
    – do not → don't
    – does not → doesn't
    – did not → didn't

📝 ПРАКТИЧЕСКИЕ ПРИМЕРЫ

Have как основной:
    – I have two cats. → Do you have pets? → Yes, I do. / No, I don't.
    – She has a problem. → Does she have a problem? → Yes, she does. / No, she doesn't.
    – They had dinner. → Did they have dinner? → Yes, they did. / No, they didn't.

Have как вспомогательный (Perfect):
    – I have finished. → Have you finished? → Yes, I have. / No, I haven't.
    – She has called. → Has she called? → Yes, she has. / No, she hasn't.
    – They had left. → Had they left? → Yes, they had. / No, they hadn't.

Do как основной:
    – I do my homework. → Do you do homework? → Yes, I do. / No, I don't.
    – She does yoga. → Does she do yoga? → Yes, she does. / No, she doesn't.
    – They did everything. → Did they do everything? → Yes, they did. / No, they didn't.

Do как вспомогательный:
    – I work here. → Do you work here? → Yes, I do. / No, I don't.
    – She speaks English. → Does she speak English? → Yes, she does. / No, she doesn't.
    – They came yesterday. → Did they come yesterday? → Yes, they did. / No, they didn't.
