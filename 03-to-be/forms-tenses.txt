ФОРМЫ ПО ВРЕМЕНАМ: УТВЕРЖДЕНИЯ • ОТРИЦАНИЯ • ВОПРОСЫ

✅ УТВЕРЖДЕНИЯ

• Прошедшее (Past Simple):
    – I/He/She/It was → He was tired.
    – You/We/They were → We were in London.

• Настоящее (Present Simple):
    – I am → I’m happy.
    – You/We/They are → You’re late.
    – He/She/It is → She’s ready.

• Будущее (Future Simple):
    – (I/You/He/She/It/We/They) will be → I will be ready. / She will be there soon.
        • Кратко: I’ll be, you’ll be, he’ll be…
    – (You/We/They) are going to be → They are going to be late.
    – (He/She/It) is going to be → She is going to be busy.
    – (I) am going to be → I am going to be careful.

✅ ОТРИЦАНИЯ

• Прошедшее (Past Simple):
    – I/He/She/It was not → wasn’t → I was not tired. / She wasn’t home.
    – You/We/They were not → weren’t → You were not here. / They weren’t prepared.

• Настоящее:
    – I am not → I’m not tired.
    – You/We/They are not → You aren’t / you’re not here.
    – He/She/It is not → He isn’t / he’s not busy.

• Будущее (Future):
    – (I/You/He/She/It/We/They) will not be → won’t be → They won’t be late.
    – not going to be (форма зависит от подлежащего):
        • (I) am not going to be → I’m not going to be late.
        • (He/She/It) is not going to be → She isn’t going to be late.
        • (You/We/They) are not going to be → We aren’t going to be late.

✅ ВОПРОСЫ (инверсия: форма be + подлежащее)

• Прошедшее:
    – Was I/he/she/it…? → Was it cold?
    – Were you/we/they…? → Were they surprised?
    – When/Where was…? → When were you born?

• Настоящее:
    – Am I…? → Am I late?
    – Are you/we/they…? → Are they ready?
    – Is he/she/it…? → Is she your sister?
    – Wh- + be + subject → Where are we?

• Будущее:
    – Will he/she/it be…? → Will she be there?
    – Are you going to be…? → Are you going to be on time?
    – What will the result be? / When will it be finished?
